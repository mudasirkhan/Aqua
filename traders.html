Aqua Traders

Having its office location at Madina Chowk Gow Kadal Srinagar. Mainly working as Civil & electrical Contractors and Suppliers with Defence, Govt. & Semi Govt departments. Having its own construction equipments/Plants like Excavater, JCB, Tippers, Concrete mixers, staging & shuttering material, TATA 207, etc which can be used for any kind of construction. We are also having the Bore well Rig machine which can do Bore hole size of 100 mm to 300 mm upto the depth of 300 Meters in any kind of soil. Apart from these movable equipments we are maintaining our own site lab maintained by our own Quality Control Department having various kind of lab equipments including Weigh Batcher, Compressive strength test machine, Weighing balance, Cube moulds, etc which are used by our technicians to provide the best quality work to our clients at various construction sites. Mainly handling the projects of departments like Military Engineering Services (MES), Engineering regiments & Border roads organization (BRO). We are having very talented & experienced staff of more than 60 employees including Engineers, Technicians & supervisors working at different construction sites.
